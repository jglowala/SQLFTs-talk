# COMPLETE PRESENTATION PROMPT

## Presentation Overview
This presentation focuses on diaphragm injuries, covering the essential aspects necessary for a comprehensive understanding. It aims to educate healthcare professionals on the types, causes, diagnosis, and treatment options related to diaphragm injuries.

## Content Structure and Timing
### 1. Introduction (2 minutes)
- Briefly describe diaphragm anatomy and function.

### 2. Classification of Diaphragm Injuries (4 minutes)
- Discuss different types of diaphragm injuries: traumatic vs. non-traumatic.

### 3. Causes of Diaphragm Injuries (5 minutes)
- Outline common causes including blunt and penetrating trauma, and surgical iatrogenesis.

### 4. Symptoms and Diagnosis (6 minutes)
- Highlight clinical symptoms and diagnostic methods including imaging techniques.

### 5. Treatment Options (6 minutes)
- Examine surgical and conservative management strategies.

### 6. Complications (5 minutes)
- Discuss potential complications and long-term outcomes.

### 7. Case Studies (6 minutes)
- Present 2-3 case studies illustrating different scenarios of diaphragm injuries.

### 8. Conclusion (3 minutes)
- Summarize key takeaways and provide final thoughts.

### 9. Q&A Session (5 minutes)
- Open the floor for audience questions.
